    Alentejo | Along the coast

    From the mouth of the River Sado to Zambujeira do Mar, the coast will surprise you for being such a well-preserved area, with small havens of sun and beach, hospitable people and good cuisine.

    Winetourism

    Getting to know Portugal through its wines is an authentic and original way of embracing the country's culture. To appreciate and get to know them, there's nothing like visiting the regions where they are produced during the harvest season.

    Travelling with books

    Discovering Portugal through writers... We travel for pleasure, for holidays, or for work, but regardless of the reason, taking a book is still on the list of things to put in the luggage.
